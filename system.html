How TrueLayers™ has simplified the building envelope.

Can it made to be simpler?

Twenty or more different types of membranes. Thicker, thinner, standard performance, premium performance.
Can we make it simpler?
We can!

We thought: Two membranes for the roof, two for the facade and one for the inside plus a all purpose tape.
Thats enough.

Can we make it cost less?

A premium price range, a standard price range, a budget price range. Is this really necessary?
It is not!

We thought: Let‘s have one premium performance product for each application and sell it on a pallet for the budget price. That simple.

How to proof reliablity?

We wanted to proof that it is possible to have competitive pricing and premium quality at the same time.
Trust, but verify.

That´s why all our products are tested scientifically and certified. Norm requirements are exceeded significantly.
